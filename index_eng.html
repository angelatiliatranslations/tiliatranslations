Welcome!

My name is Angela Brisotto and I am a linguist.

I have been helping individuals and organisations convey their words and ideas to a public that speaks a different language since 2019.

Combining analysis, accuracy and wordsmithery, I:

  • translate texts from Polish and English into Italian;
  • revise translations and proofread texts written in Italian;
  • and transcribe audio and video materials into Italian.

On this website you will find:

  • information about the services I offer;
  • language education resources (videos, articles and book recommendations);
  • information on how to contact me.
